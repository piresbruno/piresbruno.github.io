About

From a young age, I’ve been captivated by technology. I still remember the excitement of getting my first computer, discovering the internet, burning my first CD, and exploring Linux in the '90s. In the early 2000s, encountering an iBook G3 sparked a passion that continues to drive me today.

In secondary school, I immersed myself in the world of electricity and electronics. Although I began my higher education in Electrical Engineering, I soon realized that my true calling was in the digital realm, which led me to transition to Computer Science.

I could list a myriad of accomplishments, lessons learned from mistakes, and a mix of soft and hard skills developed over the years. However, the quality that defines me most is my relentless desire to learn and grow every day. I’m not the type to rely solely on memory; rather, I thrive on actively seeking solutions and gaining new insights through hands-on problem solving. My drive to make things happen has been the cornerstone of my personal and professional journey.
